Bio-Inspired Innovation

🔄

Event-Driven Activation

Dormant by default; awaken only on significant inputs — mirroring the sundew plant's efficient prey capture.

🌡️

Temperature-Controlled Gating

Soft probabilistic gating for exploration; anneal to hard thresholding for deployment.

Adaptive Threshold Control

PI-style controller with energy pressure balances target activation rate against available resources.

🔋

Transparent Energy Model

Technical Overview

Three-phase pipeline: Dormant → lightweight Evaluation → Deep Processing when the significance score exceeds an adaptive threshold.
